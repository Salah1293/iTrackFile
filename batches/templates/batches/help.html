iTrackFiles Help Page

Login Page

To access the iTrackFiles application, you need to log in with your credentials on the login page.

After logging in, you will be directed to the landing page where you can search for files and records.

Capture

The Capture section allows you to create new batches and manage existing ones. Use the top menu bar to navigate.

New Batch

Click on the "New Batch" button to open the capture form screen.

Incomplete Batches

Click on the "Incomplete Batches" button to view and continue working on unfinished batches.

Scanner Setting

Click on the "Scanner Setting" button to configure the scanner settings.

Search

Use the search functionality to find specific records. You can filter and sort results based on different criteria.

Search Results

Click on any section (e.g., Civil) to view the search results in a table format.

Double-click on any row to access options like First, Next, Previous, Print, Rotate, Reset, Edit, and Delete.
